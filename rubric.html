How each team will be evaluated for the project.

Project Evaluation Rubric

Category Description Points
Impact & Usefulness Does the project solve a meaningful problem or provide real value? Would people realistically use it? 20 pts
Creativity & Innovation Is the idea fresh, original, or an inventive spin on existing solutions? 15 pts
Technical Implementation Does it function properly? Is the solution stable, effective, and thoughtfully built (relative to time/experience)? 15 pts
Design & User Experience Is the project intuitive, clear, visually appealing, and accessible for a wide audience? 10 pts
Presentation & Communication Did the team clearly explain their project, show its purpose, and present confidently/enthusiastically? 10 pts
Teamwork & Effort Did the team collaborate well, divide tasks, stay engaged, and put in consistent effort? 10 pts
Practicality & Sustainability Could this project realistically be maintained, scaled, or improved beyond the hackathon? 10 pts
